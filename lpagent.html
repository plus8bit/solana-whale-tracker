What the agent does

It turns LPAgent pool and LP wallet metrics into a practical signal: which pools are worth monitoring, which LP wallets look skillful, and where risk is too high.

1. DiscoverCalls LPAgent pool discovery with filters for Solana/Meteora pools, organic score, liquidity, and fee opportunity.
2. RankScores pools by fee/TVL, volume, organic score, liquidity, and risk flags like low organic score or extreme volatility.
3. WatchFor the best pool, pulls top LPers and marks wallets to add back into Solana Whale Tracker watchlists.
